A Spring-singing extravaganza

Songbird was a five-week game project based on the theme "spring". The other designer on the project and myself have musical interests that we wanted to put towards a well-made audio game.

My work on the project included composing the first two songs in the game with GarageBand. I also generated three note layouts with scaling difficulty for each track. I also played a role in layout design and made sure the game would be playable by those who may be colorblind. A unique feature of Songbird is that each songbird represents an instrument in the song. This adds an "instrument switch" mechanic that adds another layer of complexity to rhythm games. Not only do you have to hit the right notes, but you have to be using the correct songbird as well. Failure to do so will cut that instrument out of the song until it is played correctly.
